We take your privacy seriously, and we give you full control over your data.

menurize does not share or sell your data to anyone.
If you create an account on menurize, we will collect some personal data, such as your email address and name, for logging you in to your account on menurize and any relevant communication to you about your account. We use Sign in with Google and Facebook login for the web, to sign you in to menurize.
If you don't create an account, we won't collect any personal data about you. However, if you want to upload your menu on menurize, you will need to create an account.
You can also delete your entire account. When you delete your account with menurize, personal data along with your menurize account, will be immediately and permanently deleted from our servers. Should you choose to come back and create and account on menurize, you will be creating a completely new account.
Should you have any questions about your privacy, we will be happy to answer them. Email us at enquiry@menurize.com.
This version of our privacy policy took effect on 15 January 2022.
